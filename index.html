About Vazento

Vazento is a global digital marketplace created to connect independent sellers with customers worldwide. Our platform allows sellers to showcase their products while receiving payments directly from their customers, ensuring simplicity and independence. We are committed to creating a space where businesses of all sizes can grow, while customers can discover unique products from different parts of the world. Vazento does not handle or control payments between sellers and customers. All transactions are conducted directly between both parties. For international orders, shipping arrangements are handled through Vazento and must be paid for accordingly.
